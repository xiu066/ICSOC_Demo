Instructions

  1. Drag & Drop
  2. Click one of a node's endpoints and hold the arrow to connect to another endpoint
  3. Double click to rename a node
  4. Double click to remove a connection
  5. Hover mouse on a node, click "x" to remove it
  6. Hover mouse on a transition, click "star" to highlight it
  7. Click "Verify" button to check your model
  8. If your model is correct, the "Verify" button will be replaced by "Next" button
  9. You'd better not to change your model after "Verify" button being replaced
  10. Click the "Next" button to check the next marking of the net
  11. The "postmark" of each token in current marking is shown in the right column

Restrictions

Petri Net Rules

A net must consist of places, transitions, and arcs. Arcs can run from a place to a transition or vice versa, but never between places or between transitions.

Control Flow Pattern Rules

  1. The process must have only one pair of starting and ending places.
  2. Starting/ending place must have only one output/input arc.
  3. Each white node (non-starting/ending places and the task transitions) must have only one input arc and one output arc.
  4. The process structure must follow the Control Flow Patterns.

Result Interpretation

  1. PM(tokenX) = ... : the postmark of token X is ...
  2. I : initial postmark
  3. A>B : B executes after A
  4. A^B : no sequence relation between A and B
  5. (A^B) : A and B execute in parallel
  6. [A^B] : A and B are in two mutually exclusive branches
  7. {A} : A executes in loop
